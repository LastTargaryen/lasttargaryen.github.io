🎵 Interactive Songwriting Template

Quick Start: Enter your song title, then click "Add Section" to begin. Use the "Rhyme Scheme Setup" button to create custom rhyme patterns with color-coded lines (A=Green, B=Blue, C=Red, D=Orange, E=Purple, F=Cyan, X=Black).

Pro Tips: When using Rhyme Scheme Setup, type letters to pre-define your pattern (e.g., A, B, A, B). Press Enter in any lyrics box to add a new line. Press Tab to jump to the next lyrics box (skipping rhyme markers).

Export: Use "Export to PDF" for a formatted print version or "Copy to Clipboard" to paste your song elsewhere.
